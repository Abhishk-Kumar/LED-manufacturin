Welcome to JONA LED

Jona Led is a renowned brand, offering a wide range of LED Display Screens to enhance your resolution. We are located in the heart of the nation, Delhi and provide our services across the nation. Jona Led has emerged as a leading LED Display maker and is known for its quality products and services. We are dedicated and committed towards creating, manufacturing, and selling the best LED products, including indoor/outdoor LED displays for fixed/rental applications, curved LED video walls, fine pixel pitch displays, and LED van displays.

Illuminating Excellence in LED Display Solutions for more than 30 years in India and overseas. We continue to push boundaries in technology and customer satisfaction, delivering unparalleled solutions that make a difference.
