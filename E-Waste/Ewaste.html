DO’s

  • Always look for information on the catalogue with your product for end-of life equipment handling in your region i.e India
  • Ensure that only Authorized Recyclers in India repair and handle your electronic products.
  • Always call our E-waste Authorized Collection points to dispose products that have reached end-of life.
  • Always drop your used electronic products batteries or any accessories when they reach the end of their life at your nearest Authorized E-waste Collection point.
  • Always disconnect the battery from product and ensure any glass surface is protected against breakage.

DON’T’s

  • Do not dismantle your electronic products on your own.
  • Do not throw electronics in bins having Do not Dispose” sign.
  • Do not give e-waste to informal (Kabbadi) and unorganized sectors like Local Scrap Dealer Rag Pickers in India.
  • Do not dispose your product in garbage bins along with municipal waste that ultimate reaches landfills.
